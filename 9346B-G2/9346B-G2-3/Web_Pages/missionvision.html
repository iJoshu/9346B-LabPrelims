The Best Bud was created because we belief that animals are entitled to kind and respectful treatment at the hands of humans and must be protected under the law. We believe that animals deserve to be loved because Animals are Friends.

MISSION

The Best Bud's mission is to provide and facilitate leadership on matters relating to the prevention of cruelty to animals and the promotion of animal welfare especially in Legarda-Burnham-Kisad Barangay.

VISION

The Best bud's vision to be the recognized authority on animal welfare issues and making a measurable difference for animals. The Best Bud is an integral part of each community, promoting mutually beneficial human-animal interactions, and is viewed as a desirable organization for volunteerism and support.
