About Animal Welfare

What is Animal Welfare?

Animal welfare means how an animal is coping with the conditions in which it lives.

An animal is in a good state of welfare if (as indicated by scientific evidence)

it is healthy, comfortable, well nourished, safe, able to express innate behavior,

and if it is not suffering from unpleasant states such as pain, fear, and distress.
